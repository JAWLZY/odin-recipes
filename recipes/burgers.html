Classic Burgers

Description

A burger is a sandwich, usually made from cooked ground beef patties, that is typically served between two pieces of bread called hamburger buns. Burgers can be variously topped and garnished to the consumer's liking.

Ingredients

  • One egg
  • One half teaspoon salt
  • One half teaspoon ground black pepper
  • One pound ground beef
  • On half cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together egg, salt and pepper. Place ground beef and bread crumbs into the mixture. With hands or a fork, mix until well blended. Form into 4 patties approximately three-fourths inch thick.
  3. Place patties on the prepared grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
